Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy describes how the Tradeshow Passport Raffle app (“the App”) collects, uses, and retains personal information when you participate in an event raffle.

Information we collect

  • Registration: name, email, phone number, industry role
  • Raffle activity: booth scan timestamps, raffle entry status
  • Authentication: email address used for magic-link sign-in
  • Administration: audit logs of admin actions (hashed identifiers)

How we use information

  • Operate the passport stamp collection and raffle entry process
  • Verify eligibility and scan progress
  • Contact winners and administer the raffle
  • Improve event operations and troubleshoot issues

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data is stored in Supabase and processed by event administrators authorized for the raffle. Service providers (hosting, email) may process data on our behalf under contract.

Retention

Event data is archived after the event. Attendee personal information is deleted after a defined retention period following event archive (default: 30 days post-archive).

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may request access to or deletion of your data. Contact the event organizer or use the admin GDPR export/delete tools provided for the event.

Security

We use authenticated admin access, row-level security, signed scan tokens, and server-side write validation to protect event data.

Contact

For privacy requests related to a specific event, contact the event organizer.
